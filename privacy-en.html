Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

1. What we collect

We collect the minimum necessary to operate the app: language, basic preferences (e.g., chosen modes, volume mix), and anonymous usage events to improve stability and experience.

Data is used to provide core features (sessions, downloads), improve quality, and detect crashes. We do not build advertising profiles.

We do not sell personal data. Trusted processors (analytics/crash reporting, payments/subscriptions) operate under contract and only process data on our behalf.

You can request access or deletion of your data at any time by emailing support@sonari.app.

5. Children

Sonari is not intended for children under the minimum age required by local law. If you believe a child used the service, contact us for deletion.

We may update this policy; material changes will be highlighted here.
