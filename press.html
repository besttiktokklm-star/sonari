Short Description

EN: “Sonari is an intelligent audio app that generates music and ambient soundscapes to help you focus, relax, and sleep — with a minimalist, distraction-free interface.”

PT: “O Sonari é uma app de áudio inteligente que gera música e paisagens sonoras para te ajudar a focar, relaxar e dormir — com uma interface minimalista e sem distrações.”

Long Description

Sonari blends generative layers and subtle variations to reduce habituation and keep you in flow longer than static playlists. Modes include Focus, Reading, Relax and Sleep. Offline packs let you use Sonari without internet. Privacy-first by design.
